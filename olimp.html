Characters (my favorites)

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" or simply "Straw Hat" ("Mugiwara"), is the main character of the anime and manga One Piece. The founder is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, as well as one of the four best fighters.
Roronoa Zoro - pirate, former bounty hunter and one of the main protagonists of One Piece.
Nami is a pirate and navigator in the Straw Hat Pirate team. She officially became the third crew member and the second to join the team after Luffy's victory over Arlong.
Vinsmoke Sanji, also known as "Black Foot" Sanji is a pirate and cook in the Straw Hat Pirates team, former chef of Barati Restaurant.
Usopp is a pirate and sniper in the Straw Hat Pirates team, as well as a former captain of Usopp's Pirates.
Tony Tony Chopper (also known as "Cotton Candy Lover") is a pirate and a doctor in the Straw Hat Pirates team.
Nico Robin is a pirate and archaeologist on the Straw Hat team. She first appeared as vice President of Baroque Works under the pseudonym Miss All Sunday.
"Soul King" Brook is a pirate and musician, a member of the Straw Hat Pirates team.
"Knight of the Sea" Jimbei is a member of the Straw Hat Pirates, where he is also the helmsman. At the moment, he is officially the tenth member of the Straw Hat Pirates and the ninth to join Luffy's team.
